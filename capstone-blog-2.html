What's New?

Following up from last post, my group for the international student experience story has began recording audio and video for our interviews. We have set our selves up in a small green screen room to record. We chose the green screen room because the audio is pretty good and the green screen gives us a consistent background when recording. We have also obtained media release forms for the interviewees. Additionally we decreased the amount of interviews we hoped to capture from 12-16 to around 10. We made this cut due to the time we have left for editing and recording.

Currently in my personal project I have put together three songs to create an EP. These songs I have classified as ambient synth-wave. Figuring out what I wanted to create was definitely a challenge for me as I have many ideas but struggle to execute them. Coming very soon I will work on the disc covers as well as the album cover. I have a profile picture I would like to use, I just have to find it. Each of the disc covers as well as my PFP will be created using illustrator. I’m not sure right now what I will make for the album cover. I would like it to be a photo though.
